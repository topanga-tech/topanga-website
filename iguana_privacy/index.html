Iguana Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 13, 2025

Iguana: Instagram Unfollowers, made by Topanga, is an Instagram analytics app that alerts users when someone unfollows them, blocks them, or any other activity on their account. Contact us at iguana.alerts@gmail.com.

Iguana is an app that helps users track Instagram follower and following activity based on publicly available profile information. This Privacy Policy explains how Iguana collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app.

Information Collection and Use

Iguana does not require login or access to your Instagram account. We only collect publicly available Instagram profile data, including:

  • Instagram username provided by the user.
  • Public follower and following lists.
  • Profile changes such as bio updates, profile picture changes, and username modifications.
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience.

Data Storage and Security

All collected data is stored either locally or in a secure and proprietary database. We do not share or sell this information to third parties, and we implement appropriate security measures to protect user data.

Third-Party Services

Iguana may use third-party services for analytics and advertising. These services operate under their respective privacy policies, and we do not share any user-provided data with them.

Children's Privacy

Iguana is not intended for users under the age of 12. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 12. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of Iguana signifies your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at iguana.alerts@gmail.com.
